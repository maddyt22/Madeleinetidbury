Billy's Brew

Packaging Design

Billy’s Brew is a local craft brewery in Noosa. A passion for surfing and beer and the desire for a local brew, Billy built his brand on the philosophy that beer is for sharing. Billy’s is more than just a brewery, but rather, a binding force bringing people together.

As part of my studies, I was tasked with designing labels for their new India pale ale, Wild Betty. Wild Betty delivers the classic punch of an IPA, with a firm bitterness, wrapped in tropical aromas, and balanced out with flavours of caramel malt and orange.
